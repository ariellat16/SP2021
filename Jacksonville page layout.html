Jacksonville, Florida




#7. Guana Reserve Beach
The Guana Reserve Beach is a favorite among Ponte Vedra residents.
After paying a parking fee of three dollars per vehicle, visitors can go to the beach,
enjoy beautiful nature trails at the Deep Creek State Forest, go canoeing, and many other activities.
The GTM Research Reserve Visitor Center, Beach Access Parking Lots,
and Trails can be found on A1A, south of Jacksonville and north of St. Augustine.


#6. Big Tablot Island State Park
Located on State Road A1A North Jacksonville, Fl, Big Talbot Island State Park
is open from 8 a.m. to sundown every day of the year.Individuals can enjoy boating, fishing, hiking,
paddling, biking, and may make a reservation to camp on the grounds.


Five Points
Five Points is loved by Jacksonville residents for its history, culture, art,
entertainment, dining, and shops. This bohemian neighborhood is known for its delicious restaurants,
coffee and dessert cafes, vintage stores, and is home of the Sun-Ray Cinema.


Riverside Arts Market
Open Saturdays from 10 to 3, the Riverside Arts Market is a local farmers market
that is popular for its local produce, art, jewelry, coffee, and baked goods.
It is located in Downtown Jacksonville under the bridge. If lucky enough, there may be live yoga
at 9 am, check out their facebook page to see the dates!
